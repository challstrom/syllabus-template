Applications

  1. (*) I can find the equation of the tangent line to a graph of a function at a point. I can interpret the linear approximation graphically and use it to estimate function values and error.
  2. (*) I can use information about a function and its derivatives to describe its behavior, including its concavity and the shape of its graph.
  3. (*) I can use derivates to solve optimization problems.
  4. I can use derivatives to solve related rate problems.
